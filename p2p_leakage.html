9. CONCLUSIONS AND RECOMMENDATIONS

9.1. Investigation Conclusions

CRITICAL FINDING: User Kamryn engaged in unauthorized P2P file sharing and distribution of confidential materials, resulting in intellectual property theft.

ESTABLISHED FACTS:

  • Unauthorized installation and use of uTorrent P2P client
  • Downloading of pre-release confidential content (Sample-1.mp3)
  • Distribution of confidential files to external email recipients
  • Systematic search for torrent trackers and additional content
  • Attempted concealment of activity (files in AppData, backup copies)

9.2. Recommended Actions

  1. Immediate Response: Disable user account, secure equipment
  2. Technical Controls: Block P2P traffic at firewall, implement DLP system
  3. Policy Enforcement: Review and reinforce information security policies
  4. Employee Training: Conduct security awareness training
  5. Legal Consultation: Assess potential legal consequences of data leakage
  6. Enhanced Monitoring: Implement network activity monitoring for similar incidents
